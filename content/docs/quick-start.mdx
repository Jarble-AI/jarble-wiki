## Prerequisites

- A Jarble account at [jarble.ai](https://jarble.ai)
- An LLM API key (Anthropic, OpenAI, or OpenRouter)

## Step 1: Create a Deployment

From the dashboard, click **New Deployment**. The onboarding wizard will guide you through:

1. **Name your bot** — Give it a descriptive name
2. **Select a runtime** — Choose OpenClaw (recommended for most use cases)
3. **Configure LLM** — Enter your API key and select a model

## Step 2: Deploy

Click **Deploy** and wait for your bot to spin up. You'll see the status change from "creating" to "running" on the dashboard.

<Callout type="warning">
First deployment may take 1-2 minutes while dependencies install. Subsequent restarts are much faster.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Chat

Once running, click your deployment to open the web chat interface. Start chatting with your bot!

## Step 4: Connect Channels (Optional)

From the deployment configuration panel, you can connect messaging platforms:

- **Telegram** — Enter your bot token from @BotFather
- **Discord** — Enter your bot token from the Discord Developer Portal
- **Slack** — Enter your bot and app tokens
- **WhatsApp** — Scan the QR code to pair
