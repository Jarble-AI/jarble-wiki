## Overview

This tutorial covers production best practices for your Jarble deployment — from choosing the right LLM configuration to monitoring your bot's health.

## Choose the Right Model

For production bots, consider:

| Model | Best For | Cost |
|-------|----------|------|
| Claude Sonnet 4 | General purpose, fast responses | Medium |
| Claude Opus 4 | Complex reasoning, accuracy-critical | High |
| GPT-4o | Multi-modal, broad knowledge | Medium |

<Callout type="info">
You can change your model at any time from the deployment configuration without redeploying.
</Callout>

## Monitor Your Bot

Check your deployment's health from the dashboard:

- **Status badge** — Running, stopped, or error state
- **Resource metrics** — CPU and memory usage
- **Uptime** — How long since last restart

## System Prompt Best Practices

Your bot's system prompt (the "soul") defines its personality and capabilities:

1. **Be specific** — Clearly define the bot's role and boundaries
2. **Include examples** — Show the format you expect for responses
3. **Set guardrails** — Define what the bot should and shouldn't do
4. **Test iteratively** — Refine based on real conversations
